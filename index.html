Who I am

Background

I was first introduced to writing code when I had to take an Intro to Computer Science class as part of my Electrical Engineering major at the University of California, Riverside. I immediately fell in love and switched my major. The curriculum mostly focused on C++, data structures and algorithms and I also took some classes in Networks, Operating Systems, Databases and more.

Despite the great education I was getting at UCR, I felt like there was so much more to learn. So I started teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript--just enough to learn how to develop and sell Google Chrome Extensions. My extensions reached thousands of users and I developed a passion for creating beautiful, user-friendly applications.

Currently

For 5 years at MZ Game Studios, I developed UIs for some of the biggest mobile games ever including Game of War and Final Fantasy. Recently the company restructured and my position was impacted. I took the opportunity to update my knowledge by taking the App Academy course again but self-paced. Upon finishing the course, I revamped my original Chillflix project which highlights my new skills and knowledge in the field.

In my work experience and personal projects, I've solidified the idea that I love developing content pixel-perfect to the specs. I really enjoy working with React/Redux to create responsive web pages with predictable state. Now I'm currently seeking Full-Stack/Frontend Developer roles in the San Francisco Bay Area.
